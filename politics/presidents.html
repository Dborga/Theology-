Separation of Church and State

The need for leaders and symbols is woven into human nature; we yearn for things to worship and ideals to uphold. But I believe we must be discerning about where we place that trust. As I examine the symbols and alliances shaping our world, I can't help but question if we’re not being led toward something other than what we’re promised—a unity not of truth and liberty but of ancient, hidden influences that quietly guide the course of nations.
